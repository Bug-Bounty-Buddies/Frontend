How It Works?

SnapSign is an advanced traffic sign recognition system designed for real-time detection and classification of road signs while driving, providing auditory feedback to the driver. The system leverages object detection techniques to identify traffic signs within video frames captured by OpenCV. Once detected, the signs are classified using a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) into predefined categories. The classified output, which is in the form of text, is then synthesized into speech using the pyttsx3 text-to-speech engine. This real-time auditory feedback enhances driver awareness and promotes overall road safety.

Steps to Use:

1. Click on the open camera button. The camera gets open.
2. Bring any traffic sign image in front of the camera.
3. The model recognizes the traffic sign and provides the text output on the screen along with an audio output.
